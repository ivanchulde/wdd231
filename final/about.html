ABOUT

History

ANIV Missionary Store was established to provide missionaries and their families with easy access to essential supplies and resources online. Starting as a small e-commerce platform, we have grown to offer a wide range of products and support services tailored to the unique needs of the missionary community.

Over the years, ANIV has expanded its services to include a comprehensive online store, offering a wide selection of missionary supplies and resources. Our platform is designed for convenience, allowing customers to browse, order, and receive products efficiently. Today, ANIV continues to innovate, making missionary shopping accessible and reliable for everyone.
