W05 Project: Website Planning Document

Site Name

Description: ANIV Store is dedicated to providing essential products and supplies to missionaries in Ecuador, making it easier for them to access what they need while serving.

Possible Domain: anivstore.com.ec

Site Purpose

  • Sell products to missionaries serving in Ecuador.
  • Offer a list of products they can buy during seasons or special dates.
  • Manual of how to buy.
  • Contact page.

Scenarios

  • What types of products ANIV offer?
  • How can I buy items?
  • Where is ANIV located and what are the phone numbers?

Color Schema

Primary Color: #0D1B2A — Used for the header and footer to provide a strong, professional look.

Secondary Color: #778DA9 — Applied to secondary elements for visual contrast and emphasis.

Accent Color: #415A77 — Used for navigation and highlights to draw attention to important areas.

Background Color: #E0E1DD — Sets a clean, light background for the main content, ensuring readability.

White: #FFFFFF — Used for text in the header and footer for clear contrast against the dark background.

Typogaphy

The primary font for this website is Roboto, which will be used for both headings and body text to ensure a clean and modern appearance.

  • Headings: Roboto, sans-serif, bold or medium weight
  • Body Text: Roboto, sans-serif, regular weight

Additional fonts may be used for special sections if needed, but Roboto will be the main typeface throughout the site for consistency and readability.
